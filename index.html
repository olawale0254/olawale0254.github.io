Biography

Abimbola Olawale is a seasoned AI Engineer with a diverse skill set in Statistical Machine Learning, Recommendation Systems, Information Retrieval, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision. He is deeply passionate about the application of Artificial Intelligence and has made significant contributions to projects related to Machine Learning, Object Detection, Semantic Extractions, and Recommendation Systems.

Abimbola boasts over four years of experience in the field of Data and has held notable positions as an AI Engineer and Researcher at Data Scientists Network (Data Science Nigeria) and as an AI Engineer/Data Scientist at Creative Advanced Technologies. He is open to collaboration on projects and startups that have the potential to positively impact both business and society.

If you are interested in discussing topics such as Computer Vision, Recommendation Systems, Geospatial Analytics, Data Science, Machine Learning, Data Engineering, or Deep Learning, Abimbola would be happy to engage in conversation with you.

In my free time, I enjoy indulging in activities such as listening to music, playing video games, and traveling.
